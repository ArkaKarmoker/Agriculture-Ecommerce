Mission: To provide technology and services to farmers, merchants, and farm laborers to help them expand their business and provide them with a wider market. Our aim is to enhance agricultural productivity through innovative solutions that simplify farming processes and make them more efficient. We are committed to improving current farming practices by offering cutting-edge tools, training, and resources tailored to the needs of our users. Additionally, we strive to educate farmers about the latest agricultural trends, sustainable practices, and market demands, empowering them to make informed decisions. Through our platform, we seek to create a supportive community where farmers can share experiences, access vital information, and connect with potential buyers, ultimately leading to greater economic stability and growth for all stakeholders involved.

Vision: To provide a helping hand to the farmers and farm laborers in improving their lives through the medium of technology, thereby improving the Agricultural Sector in the Indian Economy.

  • ‘AGRO' is a model farmer management website application.
  • ‘AGRO’ provides a concept of virtual agricultural trade to its users.
  • It helps the wholesalers and retailers in buying produce from a larger number of farmers.
